Our Story

Discover the journey behind India's most trusted education platform

Transforming Education Since 2010

Founded by a group of passionate educators, EduMentor began with a simple mission: to make quality education accessible to every student in India, regardless of their location or background.

What started as a small tutoring center in Mumbai has now grown into a nationwide network of over 850 expert educators, serving thousands of students across 25+ cities.

Our unique approach combines traditional teaching values with modern technology, creating a learning environment that is both effective and engaging. We believe in nurturing not just academic excellence but also critical thinking, creativity, and confidence in our students.

Today, EduMentor stands as a testament to what can be achieved when passionate educators come together with a shared vision of transforming India's education landscape.
