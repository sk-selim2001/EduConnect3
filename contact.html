Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us

What are your working hours?

Our center is open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. For phone inquiries, our support team is available from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM every day.

How soon can I expect a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent matters, please call our support number for immediate assistance.

Do I need to book an appointment for a campus visit?

While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend scheduling an appointment to ensure the appropriate staff members are available to assist you.

What documents should I bring for admission inquiries?

Please bring copies of your previous academic records, any standardized test scores, and a valid ID. For specific program requirements, check our website or contact admissions.

Is parking available at your center?

Yes, we have limited parking available on-site. Additional parking is available at nearby paid parking facilities.
