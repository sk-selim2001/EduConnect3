Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our classes and programs

What is the duration of each class session?

Our regular class sessions are typically 1.5 to 2 hours long. Intensive coaching sessions for competitive exams may be longer, usually 2-3 hours per session. The exact duration will be specified in the course details.

Can I change my batch or timing after enrollment?

Yes, you can request a batch change based on availability. We allow one free batch change request per month. Additional changes may be subject to availability and administrative charges.

What is the student-teacher ratio in your classes?

We maintain an optimal student-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention. Regular school curriculum classes have 10-15 students, while competitive exam batches have 15-20 students. For more personalized attention, we also offer one-on-one tutoring options.

Do you provide study materials and practice tests?

Yes, all our courses include comprehensive study materials, worksheets, and regular practice tests. For competitive exam preparation, we also provide previous years' question papers, mock tests, and performance analysis reports.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 7-day refund policy from the date of enrollment if you're not satisfied with our services. After attending more than 2 classes, only a prorated refund will be provided. Refund requests must be made in writing to our support team.
